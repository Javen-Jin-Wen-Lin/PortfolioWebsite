Projects

I have a diverse range of multidisciplinary projects spanning mechanical design, robotics, web development, and video game development. These include both academic and personal work, although not all of the projects are currently hosted on GitHub.

Active Aerodynamic System for Road Vehicles

A custom air dam for a Suburban adjusts its area of effect automatically to enhance fuel efficiency. Various designs were assessed based on aerodynamic properties and physical testing to ensure optimal performance.

Design of Depth Camera Robot

A depth camera robot utilizes a depth camera to scan 3D environments and generate point cloud data, which can be applied in various ways, such as measuring distances between objects or creating detailed 3D maps.

Design of Mobile LIDAR Robot

A mobile robot equipped with advanced sensors autonomously collects environmental data to generate a detailed virtual map, using its relative position and real-time updates to navigate efficiently toward a designated location by calculating optimal paths, avoiding obstacles.

Design and Analysis of a Driverless Delivery Vehicle

An autonomous vehicle designed for package delivery without the need for a human driver. Extensive motion analysis was conducted to optimize the vehicle's movement, influencing key design considerations.

Virtual Robot Arm

The robot arm is a highly versatile and precise piece of technology designed for a variety of automated tasks. With its advanced control systems and flexible range of motion, it excels in performing complex operations with remarkable accuracy and efficiency.
